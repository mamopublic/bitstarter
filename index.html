You can decide just how green that thing is ...

You are shopping for groceries. Or clothes. Among so many equal choices, what to pick? What you like, of course. And if you do not feel strongly one way or another? Let GentlerTouch guide you along. Just scan the barcode and you'll get some feedback about the environmental footprint of the product or the company. It comes in especially handy when alternatives are available and we can make suggestions.

Choose what fits you best, but stay informed. It matters!

In case that other products are available, it is up to you to decide whether you'll stick with your original pick or try to grow greener (it may be cheaper, too). We do not to break the bank, we just want to give it some thought. Stay informed!

FAQ

What is the purpose of this app?
It aims to make it easier to buy products (or brands) with a lighter environmental touch.
How can I help?
If you find something we did not cover, go to our webpage, attach the barcode picture and fill out a short evaluation. Users like you help to start coverage for new products.
Why can't I find information about Most Awesome Widget, Massively Cruchy Dried Watermellon, etc.?
We are constantly updating our database, including by enrolling the help of users just like you. That being said, we aim to help classify the most commonly used products; there always will be companies/products/niche items which we will not cover.
